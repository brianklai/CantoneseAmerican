# Weekly Review

Run this once a week after the latest scene has been live long enough to learn from.

## Weekly rhythm

1. Review TikTok comments.
2. Review website nominations.
3. Review analytics.
4. Pick the next scene.
5. Move one candidate to `researching`.
6. Move one researched scene to `ready-to-film`.
7. Publish one scene.
8. Update `docs/content-calendar.md`.
9. Add archive page links and social URLs after posting.
10. Record what worked.

## What to look for

- Which scenes got actual conversation instead of passive views.
- Which social hooks drove clicks back to the archive.
- Whether nominations are clustering around one category or one era.
- Whether official-source clicks are high enough to suggest a media-state adjustment.
- Whether the next backlog pick should optimize for speed, depth, or variety.
